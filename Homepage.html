Beta Beer Dye League

About the League:

The Beta Beer Dye League (BBDL) began in the spring of 2022 as a fun and competitive gathering of friends with a shared passion for the game. Now entering its fourth season, the league has grown into a staple tradition, showcasing the spirit of sportsmanship, strategy, and camaraderie. With 22 teams competing fiercely for the top spot, each season brings new rivalries, unforgettable moments, and the pursuit of glory. Under the guidance of current commissioner Mason Esworthy, the league continues to foster a welcoming community where players celebrate both the thrill of victory and the lessons of defeat. Whether it’s the nail-biting finales or the lighthearted banter among teams, the BBDL is more than just a competition—it’s a testament to shared experiences and the joy of the game.
